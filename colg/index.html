Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Skill Navigator

❓ What is Skill Navigator?

Skill Navigator is a learning platform that helps you discover skill-based roadmaps and curated YouTube content to master any topic — from coding to design.

❓ How is this different from just searching YouTube?

Instead of getting random results, Skill Navigator provides structured roadmaps and only the best video content, so you always know what to learn next..

❓ Is Skill Navigator free to use?

Yes! Skill Navigator is 100% free. You can explore roadmaps and watch all recommended videos without paying anything...

❓ Are the roadmaps beginner-friendly?

Absolutely! Each roadmap is designed to guide you step-by-step — whether you're just starting or want to go pro...

❓ What makes Skill Navigator different from other platforms?

It gives you step-by-step roadmaps with the best YouTube videos — no random content, no confusion, and 100% free.

❓ Is Skill Navigator powered by AI?

We currently offer smart curation based on user interests and topic tags. AI-based features like auto-recommendation and smart search are coming soon!.
